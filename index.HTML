Radha Krishna

The supreme personalities of Godhead

Radha Krishna

Radha-Krishna are collectively known as the combined forms of feminine as well as the masculine realities of God. Radha and Krishna are the primeval forms of God and His pleasure potency respectively . Krishna is referred to as Svayam Bhagavan, and Radha is illustrated as the primeval potency of the three main potencies of God. Radha is Krishna Himself, split into two, for the purpose of His enjoyment.
